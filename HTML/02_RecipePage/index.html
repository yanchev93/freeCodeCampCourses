Easy To Make Avocado Toast

In this recipe we will learn how to creat on of my favorite breakfast plates. Avocado Toast with an egg, berries, and topping.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Avocado - 1 piece
  • Egg - 1 piece
  • Berries - Strawberry, Blueberry, Raspberry
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • EVOO
  • Balsamic Vinegar

Instructions

  1. Toast the Bread
  2. Mash the avocado
  3. Spread the avocado on the toast
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper on the avocado toast
  5. Place the berries on top of the avocado toast
  6. Cook the egg to your desire(scrambled, over easy, over medium, or over hard) and place it on top of the avocado toast
  7. Top the avocado toast with EVOO and balsamic vinegar to your desire
